# Summary

Updated test print for bed leveling or bed warping. Default size is for the CR 10 or the CR 10s. To use, simply print and check that all four corners and middle square stick to the bed and all look similar to each other. The lines may or may not print for you depending on your slicer and settings but this is perfectly fine.
